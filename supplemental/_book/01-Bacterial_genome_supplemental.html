Chapter 1 Supplemental

This bookdown is a supplement to the main one. It contains some workflows and tools that are not part of the standard workflow workflow. However, they may prove useful depending on your needs.

The sections in this supplement will cover:

  • Running the standard workflow with multiple samples
  • Having too much sequencing coverage
  • Using long and short reads in hybrid assembly approaches
